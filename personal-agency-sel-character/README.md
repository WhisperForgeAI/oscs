# Personal Agency, Social-Emotional Learning & Character

**Layer 1 Domain**

This domain focuses on social-emotional learning, executive function, self-regulation, interpersonal skills, and the development of character and agency.

It draws heavily from the CASEL framework and related research on mindsets and metacognition.

**Related Strands (Layer 2 examples):**
- Self-Awareness & Emotional Regulation
- Self-Management & Executive Function
- Social Awareness & Empathy
- Relationship Skills & Collaboration
- Responsible Decision-Making & Agency
