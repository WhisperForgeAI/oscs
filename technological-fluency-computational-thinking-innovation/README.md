# Technological Fluency, Computational Thinking & Innovation

**Layer 1 Domain**

This domain includes computer science, digital literacy, computational thinking, media literacy, and the innovative application of technology.

It draws from ISTE Standards and CSTA K-12 Computer Science Standards.

**Related Strands (Layer 2 examples):**
- Computational Thinking
- Digital Citizenship & Safety
- Technology Systems & Tools
- Innovation & Problem-Solving
- Information & Media Literacy
