# Human Societies, History, Geography & Civic Life

**Layer 1 Domain**

This domain covers history, civics, economics, geography, and the inquiry skills needed for informed citizenship.

It draws from the C3 Framework and emphasizes disciplinary thinking, evidence use, and taking informed action.

**Related Strands (Layer 2 examples):**
- Developing Questions & Planning Inquiries
- Applying Disciplinary Concepts & Tools
- Evaluating Sources & Using Evidence
- Communicating Conclusions & Taking Informed Action
- Civic Participation & Global Awareness
