# Scientific Inquiry & Understanding the Natural World

**Layer 1 Domain**

This domain includes the three dimensions of science education: Science & Engineering Practices, Crosscutting Concepts, and Disciplinary Core Ideas (Physical Science, Life Science, Earth & Space Science).

It focuses on scientific inquiry, modeling, argumentation, and understanding natural phenomena and engineering design.

**Related Strands (Layer 2 examples):**
- Science & Engineering Practices
- Crosscutting Concepts
- Disciplinary Core Ideas
- Scientific Knowledge Building & Argumentation
