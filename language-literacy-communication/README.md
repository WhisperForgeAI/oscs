# Language, Literacy & Communication

**Layer 1 Domain**

This domain encompasses all aspects of language development, reading, writing, speaking, listening, and communication across multiple languages and modalities.

It includes foundational literacy skills, comprehension, composition, multilingual communication, and media/information literacy.

**Related Strands (Layer 2 examples):**
- Foundational Skills & Language Systems
- Comprehension & Knowledge Building
- Composition, Argumentation & Expression
- Oral Communication & Collaboration
- Research & Digital Communication
