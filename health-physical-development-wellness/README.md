# Health, Physical Development & Wellness

**Layer 1 Domain**

This domain covers comprehensive health education and physical education.

It includes skills-based health literacy (NHES) and physical activity, movement, and wellness standards (SHAPE America).

**Related Strands (Layer 2 examples):**
- Health Education Skills (accessing information, decision-making, advocacy, etc.)
- Physical Education Standards
- Wellness & Personal Health Management
